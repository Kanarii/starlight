She is a Nekomata, a feline yokai from Japanese folklore.

Picked up by Luna one day while out scavenging, Yuki arrived at the household as an amnesiac with no idea of who she was. Together we began to uncover her identity, coming to the conclusion that she is a physical manifestation of a video game character. This discovery is what led to our exploration of such a phenomenon. But with that, came chaos. Yuki has the ability to pull out characters from video games, as well as traverse the cyberspace via monitors and digital devices. This amount of power is far too dangerous for the likes of someone who lacks control over their urges. Thus, Yuki has pulled out multiple people, all of whom now live together with us as residents of the household.

Yuki is the first virtual character that Luna met and the first resident of the house.

Personality

Yuki finds it difficult to be direct with her feelings and often times switches between attitudes on a flip of a coin. It's hard to tell what her status is due to this. So a lot of people judge her based solely on her tone or mannerisms. Applying logic is difficult when you are talking to Yuki.
