Leyke

Also known as #Aurora. Leyke is a WIP name, it's not my final pick.
At night, the sky of the city shines bright as it's illuminated by an aurora borealis.
#Lightfield Technologies have created machinery capable of manipulating the Earth's magnetic field as a means to further transit between continents and planets.

This city network is known for its advanced transportation technology, revolutionising how humans traverse across the world and through the stars. Parts of the city are described as 'districts' with 'sectors' and 'junctions'. We regard a tier of the city as a zone.
There are multiple tiers extending beneath the earth; Each having its own purpose.

The zone you will see most often is the Second Zone, this is the first tier beneath the surface of the earth. It is a residential zone and is where a large majority of the city's population resides.

Zone 1

The upper most level is pretty much the face of the city, it's what they want tourists and media outlets to see and advertise. Glowing, vibrant, artificial but sleek. All the high-profile and influential people live up there as well as the most esteemed and credited... Like my father. I hate the damn place... they love looking down on the poor folk who never had a choice. It's known for its technology that can alter the magnetic fields of the planet, giving the appearance of the aurora borealis at night despite our position.

Zone 2

This is where the common people live who can't afford a high-rise up above. It's the original city from back in the early 1800s with some 'alterations' to keep up with the times. They look stuck-on, it sucks. We have parks and natural wildlife, something you'll see is missing from the upper layer. They don't care about the environment, it's just a farce. We have our own artificial atmosphere with functioning clouds which can provide rain for the soil and shade from the manmade sunlight. You move through the layers of the city using various different escalators that are heavily guarded and require all different kinds of ID and passports. Facial recognition technology makes it difficult to circumvent any of the laws that have been put in-place...

Isle of Maple

This is the town Luna and Apollo reside in.

Isle of Maple - Higher Elevation

It is situated in the Second Zone and is on the ground floor. Known for its once beautiful woodlands, it is now one of the last places where you can find natural greenery, even if in small quantities. As you traverse the ground floor, you will notice that the buildings are quite olden, dating back to the 1900s or earlier, having been preserved and renovated over the past centuries. Many additions have caused the exteriors of most buildings to be cladded in metal and new-age technology; Houses extend up, growing more complex and modern the further they reach. Through the use of escalators, you may travel between districts of the city that reside on elevated levels only accessible by the aforementioned stairway. Paths extend from houses, as if cables connecting the two, and are used to get around the town. Shopping centres are prevalent among the higher levels of the town and so are other general produce stores. As this town exists underground, there is an artificial ecosystem in-place to simulate life on the surface. The paths are mostly walkways as motor vehicles now utilise air travel. Bicyclists and other such smaller vehicles are still prevalent. All light sources are artificial.

Rynlei Street

A small cul-de-sac where Arcee and Kyrie live. It is a quaint place with not much trouble. Existing a few streets down from Luna's residence, the Loxley's have made a name for themselves as being quite eccentric; Arcee operating a small in-house art studio where she frequently invites people to visit and take part in creative events. There is also a parking lot where Kyrie practises sports-related activities.

Zone 3

This is where the factory workers live. Everyone uses robots nowadays but humans still have to do the hard work. Funnily enough a lot of people consider it a good pass time. There once was a system created where robots could handle every form of manual labour but people grew tired of having nothing to do. After several protests and riots, hiring human workers began to become acceptable once again. Air filters were fitted to prevent another global collapse from pollution, you can't really escape it when capitalism is involved. Nobody likes to discuss this layer for some reason, it's considered rude.

Zone 5

This is where all of the high-profile things go down with servers and security. There's no atmosphere and the pressure is insane from how low down it is underground. Only robots tend to go about their business down there, controlled by officials on different layers. No one knows much about it despite how easy it would be to access it in theory. Some even say a portion of the internet is stored down there in an endless void of servers. Others think it's a conspiracy and aliens actually reside down there. Who knows?
